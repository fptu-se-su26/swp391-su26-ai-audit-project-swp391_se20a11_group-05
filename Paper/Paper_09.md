# Paper 9: Edge Intelligence for Traffic Monitoring and License Plate OCR in E-Governance
- **Title**: Edge Intelligence for Traffic Monitoring and License Plate OCR in E-Governance
- **Authors**: Yuki Tanaka, Haruto Sato, Kenji Yamada
- **Journal**: Journal of Network and Systems Management
- **Year**: 2024
- **DOI**: 10.1007/s10922-024-09802-5
- **URL**: [https://link.springer.com/article/10.1007/s10922-024-09802-5](https://link.springer.com/article/10.1007/s10922-024-09802-5)

## Key Technical Contribution
Optimizes object detection (YOLO) and character recognition (OCR) models to execute directly on edge devices (Jetson Nano/mobile clients) saving up to 85% backhaul transmission bandwidth.
