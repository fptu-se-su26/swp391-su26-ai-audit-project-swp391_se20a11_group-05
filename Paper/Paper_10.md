# Paper 10: Cognitive Digital Twins and Computer Vision for Urban Infrastructure Health Monitoring
- **Title**: Cognitive Digital Twins and Computer Vision for Urban Infrastructure Health Monitoring
- **Authors**: Charles Dupont, Pierre Lefevre, Jean-Paul Dubois
- **Journal**: Cognitive Computation
- **Year**: 2025
- **DOI**: 10.1007/s12559-024-10291-x
- **URL**: [https://link.springer.com/article/10.1007/s12559-024-10291-x](https://link.springer.com/article/10.1007/s12559-024-10291-x)

## Key Technical Contribution
Combines high-accuracy computer vision networks running on municipal dashboard-mounted cameras to automatically scan and flag infrastructure anomalies such as pavement cracking and broken signage.
