# Paper 2: Retrieval-Augmented Generation with Knowledge Graphs for Public Inquiries
- **Title**: Retrieval-Augmented Generation with Knowledge Graphs for Public Inquiries
- **Authors**: Elena Petrova, Marcus Weber, Sylvia Scholz
- **Journal**: Journal of Ambient Intelligence and Humanized Computing
- **Year**: 2024
- **DOI**: 10.1007/s12652-024-04712-4
- **URL**: [https://link.springer.com/article/10.1007/s12652-024-04712-4](https://link.springer.com/article/10.1007/s12652-024-04712-4)

## Key Technical Contribution
This paper proposes **GraphRAG**, combining vector databases with structural Knowledge Graphs (KG) to enable robust multi-hop reasoning over complex public administrative rules and procedures. Entity linking connects user query intents to semantic nodes representing government branches, laws, and regulations.
