# Paper 3: Semantic Search and Intent Classification for Smart City Inquiries
- **Title**: Semantic Search and Intent Classification for Smart City Inquiries
- **Authors**: Ji-Yeon Kim, Chen Wei, Sanjay Gupta
- **Journal**: Wireless Networks
- **Year**: 2024
- **DOI**: 10.1007/s11276-023-03487-1
- **URL**: [https://link.springer.com/article/10.1007/s11276-023-03487-1](https://link.springer.com/article/10.1007/s11276-023-03487-1)

## Key Technical Contribution
Proposes a hybrid lexical-semantic retrieval engine combining BM25 keyword searches with dense vector models to accurately classify citizen query intents and route requests to correct municipal endpoints.
